About:

This app is created by Piotr Tokarski for learning and training cube algorithms. Using this app you can choose your cube, method, set and case and get the best algorithms for each situation on the cube. Also you can choose your cube, method and set for training previously known algorithms. Then, you are going to click GOOD or WRONG and all the statistic you will get below. If you forgot the algorithm for each case, you can click "Forgot?" and you will get the algorithm on the top of the site. Then, you have to click "NEXT" so as not to spoil the statistics. Also you can use shortcuts for pressing buttons responsible for evaluating the solution. I hope to improve your skills by this site.

Shortcuts:

G or g ➜ GOOD

W or w ➜ WRONG

N or n ➜ NEXT
